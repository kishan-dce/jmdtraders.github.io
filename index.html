About Jay Maa Durga Traders

Your trusted partner in building dreams since 2009

Our Story

Founded in 2020 by Pankaj Kumar Gupta, Jay Maa Durga Traders has been serving the Saharsa community for over 6 years. What started as a small hardware shop has grown into the region's most trusted one-stop destination for all building and construction needs.

Located strategically near Atalkha Chowk on the Atalkha-Patarghat Road, we have witnessed and contributed to the growth and development of countless homes, businesses, and infrastructure projects in Bihar.

Our commitment to quality, competitive pricing, and exceptional customer service has made us the preferred choice for contractors, builders, and homeowners throughout the region.
